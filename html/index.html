INTRODUCTION

Studio Architecture is registered with PACRA under the business names Act Cap 388 Laws of Zambia and under the Zambia Institute of Architects Act Cap 442 of the Laws of Zambia and also with Zambia Revenue Authority. It is with great pleasure that I am introducing Studio Architecture to you. Studio Architecture was formed primarily to offer Architectural Services which encompass, Design of buildings, Supervision of the construction process (Contractors) management of the Project cycle, Cost Control through use of alternative provable Construction methods and Urban Design. Studio Architecture also offers other services such as building renovations/restorations, Common Lease hold Schemes, Arbitration, Interior Design and Landscaping. The guiding principle for Studio Architecture is the architecture of Symbiosis which seeks to combine the best ofevery building culture in consultancy and construction methods. This principle also encourages clientparticipation in the design and hence ensures an ecstatically pleasing structure.
